Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does VocalPro serve?

+

VocalPro is specifically designed to empower professionals in hands-on fields. Our primary focus is on craftsmen (e.g., construction, manufacturing, artisans) and healthcare professionals (e.g., doctors, nurses, therapists), but our adaptable AI can be customized for other specialized sectors.

How secure is my data with VocalPro?

+

Data security is paramount. We employ end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and adhere to strict data privacy regulations, including HIPAA for our healthcare clients. Our infrastructure is regularly audited to ensure the highest standards of protection.

Can VocalPro integrate with my existing software?

+

Absolutely. VocalPro is built with robust API capabilities, allowing seamless integration with a wide range of existing CRM, EHR, ERP, and project management systems. Our team provides support to ensure a smooth integration process tailored to your specific tech stack.

What kind of support can I expect?

+

We offer tiered support based on your plan. All clients receive email support, while Pro and Enterprise plans include priority support and dedicated account managers, respectively. Our knowledge base and tutorials are also available 24/7 for self-service assistance.
